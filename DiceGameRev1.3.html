Welcome to get to '52'!

Read the rules, then click the button to start the game!


The rules:

A. 6 different sided die are rolled and the player closest to 52 wins that round;

B. Each round, you will bet if you believe you will win, lose, *TIE, or pass on betting that round;

B1. The computer will be betting on its own outcome;

C. If you guess correctly, $13.00 will be added to your score, if you guess incorrectly, $6.50 will be deducted;

C1. If you guess correctly that the round was a *TIE, you automatically win the game, but if you are wrong, $13.00 will be deducted;

C2. If you choose not to place a bet, $3.25 will be deducted;

D. Negative balances in the wallet cause the game to start over;

E. The first player to $52.00 is the winner of the game!!;

F. You and the computer have started with $13.00 so get betting!!
